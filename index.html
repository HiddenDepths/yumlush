About yumLush

yumLush gift emporium is located in the heart of sunny Gosforth in The Gosforth Centre. Offering a wide range of crafted gifts, cards, trinkets, spangles and thingy-me-bobs, a trip to yumLush is an experience for all.

One of Newcastle’s most exclusive gift shops, at yumLush we stock the work of a variety of renowned local artists, designers and makers as well as plenty of items from further afield. So come and visit us today; you’ve everything to gain!
